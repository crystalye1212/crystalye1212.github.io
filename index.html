About Crystal Glass Design

Crystal Glass Design is a professional custom glass installation company serving Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area. We provide clean, modern, and reliable glass solutions for commercial and residential projects.

Our work includes commercial glass, storefront glass, office partitions, shower glass, glass railings, closet glass doors, mirror installation, and storm / wind protection glass systems.

Our Services

Commercial Glass

Storefront, office, and commercial glass installation.

Glass Partitions

Interior glass walls and office partition systems.

Glass Railings

Glass railings for stairs, balconies, and exterior areas.

Shower Glass

Frameless and sliding shower glass installation.

Storm / Wind Protection Doors

Outdoor wind protection and entrance glass systems.

Closet Glass Doors

Modern sliding glass closet door systems.

Mirror Installation

Wall mirrors, gym mirrors, and custom mirror projects.
